Welcome to Paradise

Escape to Green Haven Resort, nestled in the serene landscapes of Suryabinayak, Bhaktapur. We offer a peaceful sanctuary where you can unwind amidst lush, meticulously maintained gardens. Ascend our unique tower lookout for breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding hills and valleys. Experience genuine Nepali hospitality and create unforgettable memories in a tranquil, nature-focused environment.
